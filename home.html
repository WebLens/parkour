TRAINING PLAN

Child

First acquaintance with parkour, learning the basic elements and safety precautions
  • 7-9 years Improved coordination, sense of balance and flexibility
  • Development of the musculoskeletal system
  • Using game methods to release excessive activity

teenager

At this age, endurance is best suited for training, also:
  • The elements being performed become more complicated, and the combinations (combinations of individual elements in continuous movement) become more diverse.
  • Interaction with surfaces located in different planes is trained.

teenager+

Each pupil forms an individual style, they are able to independently invent and make combinations and perform elements in any situation.
  • Strength Development
  • the study of acrobatic elements of increased complexity
  • The introduction of acrobatics in overcoming obstacles and its use in making combinations

Adult

This age is unlimited.:
  • We can learn everything that we ourselves know how
  • Increased load on the body
